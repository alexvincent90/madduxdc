The Maddux Method

Why my chiropractic is different.

Most clinics run a single technique on every body that walks in. I don't. Here's what shapes my care.

01

Multi-technique by design

Activator, Thompson Drop, Diversified, Gonstead, Sacro Occipital Technique — I trained in all of them so I can match the technique to the patient, not the other way around.

02

Built from lived experience

Chiropractic care brought me back from a major accident in 2021. That's not a marketing line — it's why I do this. I know what a real recovery feels like from the patient side of the table.

03

Care that compounds

Adjustments are the easy part. Movement coaching, stretching, and lifestyle tweaks between visits are what keep the gains. I'll send you home with homework, not a punch card.

04

Honest about scope

Chiropractic isn't magic and it isn't for everything. If something needs imaging, ortho, PT, or a different specialist — I'll tell you, and I'll help route you to the right person.

JM Dr. Justin Maddux, DC

My story

Why I do this work.

In May 2021 I was in a serious motor vehicle accident. The thing that brought me back wasn't surgery, wasn't medication — it was a chiropractor who actually paid attention.

That recovery rewrote my career. I went to Life West Chiropractic College, graduated magna cum laude in March 2025, and built my practice around the kind of care that put me back together.

I see patients across two Sacramento clinics. Same hands, same approach, two convenient locations.
